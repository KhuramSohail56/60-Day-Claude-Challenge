# Technical Key Learnings: Day 14 Automated Job Risk Engineering

* **Unmasking Role Inflation:** Learned how to bypass general corporate brand phrasing and evaluate the actual core workload distribution. Identifying patterns where companies try to combine multiple distinct roles (Dev + QA + Support) into a single intern slot helps avoid rapid burnout.
* **Detecting Geographic & Financial Contradictions:** Understood the mechanics of hidden operational traps, such as calling a role "100% Remote" while binding it to short-notice onsite visits, or hiding compensation to reduce candidate negotiation leverage later in the recruitment pipeline.
* **Proactive Interview Engineering:** Realized that identifying red flags isn't just about avoiding jobs, but about transforming those risks into clear, professional validation questions during screening rounds to confidently evaluate an employer's true work culture.
