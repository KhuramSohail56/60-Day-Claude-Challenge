How to Play

You're the Head of Operations for a global supply network. Alerts will stream in — congestion, delays, breakdowns, shortages. Each one is a decision.

Your Job

  • Review each alert's priority and business impact.
  • Pick the best action before its timer runs out.
  • Multiple alerts can be active at once — triage matters.

Scoring

  • Good decisions raise KPIs and your score.
  • Poor or ignored decisions hurt customer satisfaction and cost.
  • Some consequences arrive a few seconds later — think ahead.

Goal

Keep Service Level, Customer Satisfaction, Inventory Health, and Transportation Efficiency as high as possible — while protecting revenue and controlling cost — before the 3-minute shift ends.
