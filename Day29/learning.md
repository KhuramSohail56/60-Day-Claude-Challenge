## 💡 Key Supply Chain Learnings
* **Redundancy Over Low-Cost Optimization:** Learned that maintaining alternative nearshore/air freight distribution lanes is a structural necessity to prevent delivery freezes when international maritime channels lock down.
* **Information Openness Reduces Lead Time:** Discovered that establishing open, digital data integration channels with sub-tier vendors stabilizes supply lines far more efficiently than implementing strict Net-90 financial cash penalties.
* **Cascading Risk Isolation:** Mastered the concept of multi-variable risk tracking, practicing how short-term tactical movements can unintentionally degrade mid-term profit parameters if working capital rules are misapplied.
