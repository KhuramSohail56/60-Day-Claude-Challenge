⚙️ Three Practical Media Literacy Habits

• 1. Context Neutralization: Strip away loaded adjectives ("corrupt", "secretive") to isolate the core underlying verifiable action items.
• 2. Outrage Validation Circuits: When an article triggers rapid indignation, pause for a moment to evaluate the publisher's monetization/traffic incentives.
• 3. Verify Primary Repositories: Trace scientific assertions directly back to original peer-reviewed source documentation rather than aggregated social headlines.
